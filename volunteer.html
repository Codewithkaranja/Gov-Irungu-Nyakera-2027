Campaign Outreach

Help us connect with voters across Nairobi's constituencies through door-to-door campaigns, community meetings, and public forums.

  • Community mobilization
  • Voter registration drives
  • Public awareness campaigns

Event Coordination

Help organize and manage campaign events, rallies, town halls, and community meetings across Nairobi County.

  • Event planning & logistics
  • Venue coordination
  • Crowd management
  • Guest registration

Digital & Social Media

Use your digital skills to amplify our message online and engage with voters through social media platforms.

  • Social media management
  • Content creation
  • Digital campaigning
  • Online community building

Phone Banking

  • Voter outreach calls
  • Information dissemination
  • Follow-up with supporters
  • Feedback collection

Administrative Support

Help with the day-to-day operations of the campaign office and ensure smooth coordination of activities.

  • Office administration
  • Data entry & management
  • Volunteer coordination
  • Resource distribution

Logistics & Transportation

Support campaign activities by assisting with transportation, logistics, and movement of materials across Nairobi.

  • Campaign material distribution
  • Transport coordination
  • Logistics planning
  • Equipment management
